About Frugal Mail

Frugal Mail was made to solve a problem by getting important parcels traveling accross the globe to their location quickly and cheaply for American domiciled teachers and expats in Asia.


How the system works is the "From Person" mails cheaply and domestically via USPS (or hand delivers) to the courier("a person who is going on an international flight to China"). The courier than completes the flight and meets up with the "To Person", ideally the "To Person" or "From Person" tips the courier. If they cannot meet, dropping off the package with the local mailing system should work.


This website has been developed by Rob Birch a former ESL teacher in South Korea. Please be cautious on what is shipped as the creater of this website assumes no liability.
